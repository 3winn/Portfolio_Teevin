UX / UI · SaaS · App · 2021

Tivlog

Tivlog is a video conferencing and team scheduling platform built for Thai businesses. The product needed to go beyond just video calls — and give teams a real way to plan, track, and meet in one place.

Client
Tivlog Co., Ltd.
Role
Lead UX/UI Designer
Timeline
2021
Deliverables
UX Research · App Design · Web Platform
01 — Challenge

More than just a video call.

When remote work became the norm, Thai businesses needed collaboration tools — but most reached for international platforms that weren't built with local workflows in mind. Tivlog saw the gap.

The challenge was designing a product that could compete with Zoom and Google Meet on ease of use, while offering something they didn't — an integrated scheduling system that actually made sense for the way Thai teams work.

02 — Approach

Video. Schedule. All in one place.

01

Landing Page

A clear, confident homepage that communicated the product's value in seconds — without overwhelming visitors with feature lists.

02

Company & Team Setup

Onboarding that lets users set up their organisation and invite teammates quickly — designed to get teams to their first meeting as fast as possible.

03

Video Conference UI

A clean call interface that kept distractions out of frame — with controls that felt familiar but cleaner than the competition.

04

Schedule Management

A scheduling view that connected meetings to tasks and availability — the feature that made Tivlog more than just another video tool.

03 — Outcomes
1
Unified platform for video, scheduling, and team management
4
Core flows — home, onboarding, call, schedule
SaaS
Designed for Thai teams and local workflow patterns
