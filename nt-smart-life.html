Branding · Campaign · Key Visual · Exhibition · 2021

NT Smart Life

National Telecom was entering a new era — six smart services, one unified story. The challenge was making government infrastructure feel human, forward-thinking, and worth believing in.

Client
National Telecom (NT)
Role
Art Director · Visual Designer
Timeline
2021
Deliverables
Key Visuals · Brochure · Exhibition Identity
01 — Challenge

Six services.
One coherent brand.

NT Smart Life is an umbrella platform covering Business, Healthcare, Education, Environment, Industrial, and Tracking — six distinct services that needed to feel like siblings, not strangers.

The visual system had to work at scale: from exhibition halls to digital banners, from printed brochures to screens — all while conveying the seriousness of a state enterprise and the optimism of a digital-first future.

02 — Approach

A visual language
built to scale across six worlds.

01

Unified Color System

Each service line gets its own signature color — distinct enough to stand alone, harmonious enough to sit together. A shared typographic grid keeps them in the same family.

02

Key Visual Direction

Six campaign KVs built on a consistent visual logic: human context + smart technology + NT brand mark. From industrial sites to hospital wards — every category feels purposeful.

03

Exhibition Identity

The system was designed for real space. Large-format panels, booth backdrops, and wayfinding — all adapted from the same visual DNA without losing clarity at any size.

04

Brochure System

A branded brochure suite that communicates NT's full service portfolio — structured for sales teams, exhibition visitors, and executive presentations alike.
