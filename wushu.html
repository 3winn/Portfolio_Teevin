Crypto Wushu

Crypto Wushu is a blockchain card game. The world needed to feel epic and collectible — and the marketplace UI needed to make trading cards as straightforward as any online shop.

Client
Crypto Wushu
Role
Creative Director · UI Designer
Timeline
2022
Deliverables
Landing Page · Card Design · Character Art · Marketplace UI
01 — Challenge

A card game that earns its look.

The crypto gaming space is full of projects that look rushed — generic fantasy art, cluttered UIs, and brands that don't hold together. Crypto Wushu wanted to do it differently.

The visual world needed to be genuinely compelling — something that made players want to collect the cards, not just trade them. And the marketplace had to be simple enough that anyone could buy, sell, and understand their collection without needing to understand blockchain first.

02 — Approach

Epic visuals. Clear UX.

The design covered two very different challenges in one project: building an immersive world players wanted to be part of, and a marketplace simple enough for anyone to use.

01

Landing Page

A dramatic, cinematic landing page that introduced the Wushu world and made the game's visual ambition clear from the first scroll.

02

Card Design

A card visual system that balanced martial arts themes with collectible appeal — each card designed to feel unique and worth owning.

03

Character & Abilities

Illustrated the character lineup and designed the ability cards — building out the game's visual universe so each character had a clear identity.

04

Marketplace UI

A clean marketplace interface that made buying, selling, and browsing cards simple — no crypto jargon, no confusing flows.
