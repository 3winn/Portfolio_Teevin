About

Design that ships.

I'm Atom — Teevin Hiranlertprasert — a designer based in Bangkok. Over ten-plus years I've worked across four disciplines: product UX/UI, brand identity, graphic design and marketing content. I've led design on a government utility platform that helped grow Koh Samui's monthly revenue from ฿0.9M to ฿8M, shaped the UX of Baania's real-estate marketplace, and built a big-data platform UI for the Ministry of Culture.

The range runs wide on purpose: service apps for True and National Telecom, a business-matching expo platform for logistics, an NFT card game, brand identities for cafes in Bangkok and a hotel in Siem Reap, and five years of social content for national brands like 7-Eleven, SCG, Arabus and Weber. Different industries, same standard. Good design is not the prettiest version. It is the version that actually ships.
