Branding · Hotel · Siem Reap · 2015

Korner St.

Korner St. is a hotel on a street corner in Siem Reap, Cambodia. The window grilles at that corner became the heart of the brand: their shapes were drawn into the logo and the graphic style. Warm shades of orange carry the feeling of a relaxed stay in a tropical city.

The logo mark rebuilds the corner windows' grille shapes in layered shades of orange — light and shadow through the frames.

01 — Overview

A corner hotel, seen through its windows

Korner St. stands on a street corner in Siem Reap. The window frames at that corner give the building its character, so we made them the brand's signature. Their shapes were simplified and rebuilt as the logo mark, then extended into a graphic pattern that runs across the identity — from stationery to in-room amenities.

The palette is warm and led by orange. Many shades of it sit side by side in the mark and the pattern, like sunlight and shadow falling through the windows. The result is simple: one look at the identity and you feel the ease of a tropical stay.

The graphic style in use: the color guide and the window-grille pattern carried across amenities and stationery.
