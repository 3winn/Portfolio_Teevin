New Economy Academy

NEW ECONOMY ACADEMY is a knowledge center for sharing digital marketing, business and design knowledge from the government to small and medium enterprises.

Client
New Economy Academy (NEA)
Role
Brand Designer
Timeline
2019
Deliverables
Logo · Color System · Visual Identity · Leaflet
01 — Challenge

Government design that doesn't feel like government.

Most government branding plays it safe — institutional colors, formal type, and no personality. NEA was different. The program was genuinely trying to help people, and the brand needed to communicate that warmth and ambition from the first glance.

The challenge was finding a visual language that was credible enough for official use but human enough to actually connect with the small business owners the program was built for.

02 — Concept

Concept

A small and medium enterprise is like a bee — individually diligent, but truly powerful when working together in harmony.

NEA created this center as a hive of opportunity, designed to amplify market potential and cultivate shared wisdom. Like a honeycomb, it strengthens with every entrepreneur who returns to contribute their knowledge, building a thriving academy where ideas grow and innovation flourishes.

03 — Approach

Smart, connected, and built to grow.

01

Logo Design

Two strokes interlock inside a hexagon — a mark built around connection. It reflects knowledge flowing between government and entrepreneurs, and works at any size, from official documents to a profile picture.

02

Color System

A crisp, innovative blue leads the palette — deep blue for trust, sky blue for energy, and a neutral grey for balance. Official enough for government, fresh enough for a startup classroom.

03

Graphic Style

A flexible illustration and pattern system that could be applied across touchpoints — from stage backdrops to leaflets to digital banners.

04

Leaflet & Collateral

Applied the system to printed materials used in workshops and government events — making sure every touchpoint felt consistent and intentional.

Graphic Concept

At the core of the brand lies the hexagon — a geometry of unity and resilience. By pairing this structural symbol with a crisp, innovative blue, the design communicates a vision that is both rooted in shared wisdom and propelled by future-ready technology.
